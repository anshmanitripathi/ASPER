ABOUT

All Student Portal for Educational Record is set-up as a supervising Body to conduct research and study the reason and condition of the students dropout in schools. This platform maintains the student's educational record with taking care of facilities available for further education and to promote all Student education , development in the country in a coordinated and integrated manner.

Complications

In India, the high dropout rate of students at the school level is due to several different factors. This will not only affect their personal growth but also exert an influence on the overall country's development. The lack of education will lead to the population without adequate skills, further affecting the country's productivity!

What? WHY? HOW? ASPER

What Is ASPER ?

The idea of this initiative is to improve the situation of the students who are dropping out their education, to analyze the cause , to track all the Educational journey which help further. This need to be addressed and it should be ensured that all children are enrolled in schools and that they complete their education.

Why ASPER ?

1. The dropout rate is clear indication of the difficulty in education system to retain students in school , which ultimately leads to a significant gap in achieving all student education program.

2. When children drop out of school,it not only affects their personal growth like awareness about health, future generation care , development etc but also has far reaching implications for the country's overall development.

3. The lack of education leads to the creation of population with inadequate skills,ultimately affecting the country's productivity and competitiveness ,which will be liability for country' as well as government.

4. After analysis of data Government can take essential steps and make policies to retain students like infrastructure, caste , religion , gender , awareness, area etc.
